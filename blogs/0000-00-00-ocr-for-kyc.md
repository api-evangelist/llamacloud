Standard OCR fails to meet compliance requirements for KYC workflows because it was built for clean typed documents rather than real-world identity documents with security features and varied scripts. Agentic OCR with layout-aware computer vision and self-correction loops achieves 90-95%+ straight-through processing versus 60-80% for traditional OCR.
